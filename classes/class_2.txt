Class: 
Student

Attributes: 
name (string)
grade(string)
courses_enrolled (array)
GPA (float)
course_grades (hash)
honor_roll (boolean)
extracurricular_activities (array)

Methods: 

update_grade (updates student's current grade in course_grades)
review_honor_roll_status (if students is in honor roll, then changes honor_roll to true. If student is not in honor roll, then then changes honor_roll to false)
remove_course (removes a student's course from courses_enrolled)
add_extracurricular_activity (added extracurricular activity to extracurricular_activity)
